# Cathy M. Dela Cruz — Resume Website
### A Personal Resume Website | Multimedia Development — Prelim Exam

---

## 📌 Project Overview

A personal resume website built as a **Prelim Exam project**, designed to help a real client stand out when applying for a remote **Virtual Administrative Assistant** position at a startup company in Indiana, USA.

The client, **Cathy M. Dela Cruz**, has 4 years of experience as a Customer Service Representative but has no direct experience as a Virtual Assistant. The challenge was to present her transferable skills in a convincing and professional way to make her a competitive candidate for the role.

---

## 🎯 The Challenge

Cathy is transitioning from an on-site CSR role to a fully remote VA position. The website needed to:

- Highlight her **transferable skills** from customer service (communication, problem-solving, multitasking, tools proficiency)
- Frame her experience in a way that aligns with what companies look for in a Virtual Administrative Assistant
- Present her professionally despite having **no direct VA experience**
